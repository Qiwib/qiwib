About QiwiB

QiwiB means Quantum integrator for interacting Bosons.

QiwiB is a program for Linux/MacOS to solve the many-body Schrödinger equation of interacting bosons in one dimension. It implements the MCTDHB theory [1] in an efficient way to allow effective calculations for hundreds and thousands of particles.
It's aim is to give users the opportunity even without any programming skills to simulate bosons on a quantum level.
QiwiB has been written in GNU Octave and uses its C++ extensions for more time consuming parts of the program.

Development has started in 2009 and and 2010 marked the first usable version of QiwiB and since then many more applications has been added to improve the program.

[1] O. E. Alon, A. I. Streltsov, and L. S. Cederbaum, Phys. Rev. A 77, 033613 (2008).
